Our Story

Built on Hawaiian
Values & Hard Work

AK247 is a locally owned and operated handyman service rooted in the heart of Oahu. As a Native Hawaiian business, we bring the values of our culture into every job -- respect, reliability, and pride in workmanship.

We believe your home deserves the same care and protection we give our own. Every repair, every installation, every project is handled with precision, honesty, and aloha.

From quick fixes to complete property maintenance, AK247 is the name Oahu trusts.
